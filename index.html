Avarus Invidia

Astrophysicist at Institute for Astro- and Particle Physics.

I am a PhD student dealing with high-energy astroparticle physics by means of numerical simulations. I mainly work with transport equations on the computational level, though I also have an interes in GRMHD (general-relativistc magnetohydrodynamics). In other words, I deal in CFD (computational fluid dynamics). I started my coding career with C in my second year of Master studies (as the code I was working on was written in said language) and transitioned to C++ for my PhD with a splash of python for data analysis and visualization.
